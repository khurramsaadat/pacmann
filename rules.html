üëª Meet the Ghosts

Blinky

The leader in red. Aggressively pursues Pac-Man directly. Gets faster as dots are eaten. Known as "Shadow" for his relentless pursuit.

Pinky

The pink ghost. Tries to ambush by targeting the space in front of Pac-Man. Works with Blinky to trap you. Known as "Speedy".

Inky

The cyan/blue ghost. Most unpredictable. Movement based on both Pac-Man and Blinky's positions. Known as "Bashful".

Clyde

The orange ghost. Patrols his corner and occasionally chases. Moves away when close to Pac-Man. Known as "Pokey".

Ghost States:

  • Normal: Chase or scatter based on their personality
  • Frightened: Turn blue and flee when Pac-Man eats a Power Pellet
  • Blinking: Warning that frightened state is ending soon
  • Eyes: Return to ghost house after being eaten

‚≠ê Power Pellets

Large flashing dots in corners. Eating one turns ghosts blue and vulnerable for a limited time!

Use the tunnels on the sides to quickly escape from ghosts or reach other parts of the maze.

Setup Guide

  1. Clone the repository: git clone https://github.com/khurramsaadat/pac-man.git
  2. Navigate to the project directory: cd pac-man
  3. Start a local server (e.g., using Python): python -m http.server 8000
  4. Open your browser and visit: http://localhost:8000

FAQ

Q: How do I start the game?
A: Press SPACEBAR on desktop or tap the screen on mobile devices.
Q: How do I pause the game?
A: Press ESC on desktop or use two-finger tap on mobile.
Q: What do the different ghosts do?
A: Each ghost has unique behavior: Blinky chases directly, Pinky tries to ambush, Inky is unpredictable, and Clyde patrols his area.
Q: How do Power Pellets work?
A: Power Pellets make ghosts vulnerable and blue. Eating them in this state gives bonus points.
Q: Does the game work on mobile?
A: Yes! Use swipe gestures to move and two-finger tap to pause. Fullscreen mode available.
